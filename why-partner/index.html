The Infrastructure

What surrounds every product we carry.

Twelve operational and clinical pieces that move with every product into your practice — none of them billed separately, none of them optional.

Benefit verification

Payer coverage confirmed before treatment — no surprise denials downstream, no patient financial ambush.

HCPCS & CPT coding guidance

Per-product, per-payer coding verified against current LCDs and payer policy — not a best guess from a catalog.

Documentation templates

LCD/NCD-aligned chart-note templates tuned to the specific product — audit-defensible from the first note forward.

Portal-integrated ordering

One portal for orders, shipping status, delivery confirmation — no separate logins per manufacturer, no phone-tag for reorders.

Patient utilization tracking

Per-patient product usage, outcomes, and duration-of-therapy tracking visible in real time to your clinical and compliance teams.

Claim status monitoring

Downstream visibility into paid, pending, denied, and under-appeal claims for every product we shipped — not your job to chase.

Audit-readiness

Proactive monitoring of documentation patterns and coding frequency — if a RAC or commercial audit is likely, you hear from us first.

Clinical training

On-site and remote product training per clinical pathway — structured for nurses, PAs, MDs, and the rest of the team actually using the product.

Specialty-specific playbooks

Wound care, podiatry, plastic surgery, vascular, and surgical applications each get a workflow tuned to their specialty — not a generic deck.

Regulatory updates

LCD changes, HCPCS revisions, CMS pass-through status, NCD updates — pushed to your team before they change billing, not after denial lands.

Escalation response

24-hour response on priority escalations — claim denials, prior-auth disputes, audit letters, or clinical questions mid-procedure.

Legal & compliance backstop

In-house medical-legal counsel available on every audit, complaint, and documentation gray area — included in the partnership, not re-billed.
