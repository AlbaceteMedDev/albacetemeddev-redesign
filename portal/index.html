Common questions

What to know about the portal.

What does the portal do? +
The Albacete MedDev portal is your centralized patient care and product tracking system. It connects patient-level treatment tracking, product ordering, compliance documentation, and utilization reporting in one place. Your team gets real-time visibility across every aspect of your wound care program — without juggling spreadsheets, email chains, or vendor portals.
Who has access? +
Access is granted to your clinical team with role-based permissions — physicians, nurses, documentation specialists, billing staff, and practice administrators each see the views and functions appropriate to their role. We provision access during onboarding and adjust as your team evolves.
How is data kept secure? +
The portal is built with healthcare-grade security practices — encrypted data transit, role-based access controls, audit logging, and infrastructure designed to align with healthcare privacy requirements. Security and compliance are foundational to the portal, not an afterthought. We walk through specifics during onboarding.
