What sits in the portal on day one.

Patient tracking

Every patient, every product, every visit — full treatment history in one view. Supports audit readiness and clinical decision-making.

Real-time ordering

Place product orders and follow them end-to-end — shipment, delivery, and confirmation visible without chasing emails or phone calls.

Compliance templates

LCD/NCD-aligned documentation built into the workflow. Wound measurements, SWOs, and HCPCS validation happen as you work — not as a separate step.

Utilization reporting

Physicians, nurses, documentation, billing, administrators — each team member sees the views and functions appropriate to their role.

Encrypted in transit, audit-logged, and always on — your chart stack is accessible the hour an audit or patient call lands.
