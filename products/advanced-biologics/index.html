Common questions

What to know about the biologics portfolio.

What differentiates Microlyte SAM? +
Microlyte SAM is a sub-cytotoxic silver antimicrobial dressing delivering broad-spectrum antimicrobial protection with 50–100x lower silver concentration than conventional dressings — while achieving equivalent kill rates. FDA 510(k) cleared (K153756), HCPCS A2005. Conforms fully to the wound surface, eliminates dead space, and is bioresorbable within 7 days. Clinical data shows 91% healed or improved outcomes at 12 weeks.
What is Tri-Membrane Wrap? +
Tri-Membrane Wrap is an A-C-A (amnion-chorion-amnion) tri-layer allograft offering the highest ECM concentration in the BioLab Sciences portfolio. Designed as an escalation product for failed prior grafts, high-risk patients, and complex wound presentations — Tri-Membrane Wrap delivers maximum regenerative support where conventional approaches have underperformed.
What is Membrane Wrap? +
Membrane Wrap is a dual-layer amnion allograft designed for moderate chronic wounds. With 2x the extracellular matrix (ECM) payload of single-layer alternatives, Membrane Wrap provides enhanced structural support and concentrated growth factor delivery across a broad range of wound care indications.
What is Membrane Wrap Lyte? +
Membrane Wrap Lyte is a single-amnion allograft indicated for shallow diabetic foot ulcers (DFUs) and partial-thickness venous leg ulcers (VLUs). As a cost-effective entry point into biologic wound care, Membrane Wrap Lyte delivers essential amniotic growth factors and extracellular matrix scaffolding in an accessible format.
