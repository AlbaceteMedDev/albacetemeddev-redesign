Common questions

What to know about wholesale distribution.

What does wholesale mean for our facility? +
As a medical supplies wholesaler, Albacete MedDev connects hospitals and surgical centers directly with FDA-approved wound care products at wholesale pricing. Your facility receives the same quality supplies used in leading healthcare institutions — through a streamlined procurement channel with competitive pricing, proper HCPCS coding for every product, and compliance-ready documentation that supports reimbursement from Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial payers.
How does HCPCS support reimbursement? +
Every medical supply in our wholesale catalog is assigned its correct HCPCS (Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System) code. These codes are essential for billing — they allow hospitals and surgical centers to submit claims for reimbursement to CMS and commercial payers. When paired with proper documentation of medical necessity, correct HCPCS coding ensures your facility can recover costs for the supplies used in patient care, subject to the applicable LCDs and NCDs for each product category.
Who qualifies for wholesale access? +
Wholesale distribution is available to hospitals, surgical centers, wound care clinics, and qualified healthcare organizations. Our catalog includes advanced wound dressings (foam, alginate, collagen, antimicrobial), skin substitutes and amniotic allografts, compression therapy supplies, surgical preparation products, post-operative wound management supplies, and conservative wound care staples. All products meet FDA regulatory requirements and are sourced from established manufacturers.
