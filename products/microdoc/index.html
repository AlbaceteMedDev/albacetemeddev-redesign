Common questions

What to know about MicroDoc.

How does MicroDoc work? +
MicroDoc delivers disposable negative pressure wound therapy in a portable, single-use device. It applies controlled sub-atmospheric pressure to the wound bed — supporting exudate management, promoting granulation tissue formation, and assisting wound bed preparation. Unlike traditional NPWT, there's no durable equipment to rent, clean, or track between patients.
What are the clinical applications? +
MicroDoc is indicated for a broad range of wound presentations — including chronic wounds (DFU, VLU, pressure injuries), post-surgical incision management, dehisced wounds, skin grafts and flaps, and complex wound bed preparation prior to advanced therapy application. Appropriate across outpatient clinics, home health, and transition-of-care settings.
